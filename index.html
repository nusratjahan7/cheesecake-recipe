Mom's Secret Cheesecake

This Cheesecake has been in my family for generations and never fails to delight

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 3 tbsp granulated sugar
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 24 oz (3 packages, 8 oz each) cream cheese, softened to room temperature
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup sour cream, room temperature
  • 3 large eggs, lightly beaten and at room temperature 2 cups fresh blueberries
  • 3 cups fresh or frozen blueberries, divided ¼ cup granulated sugar, 1 tbsp cornstarch, ¼ cup cold water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325F (160C). In a medium-sized bowl, stir together graham cracker crumbs, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until combined.
